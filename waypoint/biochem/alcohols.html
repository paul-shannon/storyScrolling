An alcohol is a type of organic compount that carries at least one hydroxyl -OH functional group bound to carbon.

Alcohols range from the simple, like methanol and ethanol, to complex, like sugars and cholesterol.

The presence of an OH group strongly modified the properties of hydrocarbons, conferring hydrophyllic properties.
